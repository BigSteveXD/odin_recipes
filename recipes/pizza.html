Brooklyn Style Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza made with cheese, tomato, and pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • dough
  • spices
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. Get ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  3. spread dough over pizza pan
  4. add tomato sauce and spices
  5. cover with motzerella cheese
  6. top it off with peperronis
  7. Place in oven for 15 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
